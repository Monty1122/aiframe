Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Check out answers to our most common inquiries below. If you don't see your question, feel free to contact us.

Is it really my own voice?

Yes! We model your exact voice and then refine it. Your avatar sounds just like you—but we optimize pacing and clarity for better engagement. Our AI preserves your unique vocal characteristics while removing common issues like filler words and hesitations.

How secure is my data?

We use industry-standard encryption and comply with relevant data protection laws. Only you control how you share your avatar videos. All uploads are encrypted in transit and at rest, and we never use your content to train our models without explicit permission.

Can I edit my script or audio after uploading?

Absolutely. You can revise your script or translate to multiple languages without re-recording. Our AI will regenerate your avatar video with the updated content while maintaining your visual and vocal style.

Is it possible to create a live avatar?

Currently, our platform supports pre-recorded videos. Live avatar capability is on our roadmap for future releases. We're actively working on reducing the processing time to enable near-real-time avatar generation.

How many videos can I create?

The number of videos you can create depends on your subscription plan. Our Basic plan includes 5 transformations per month, while our Pro plan offers unlimited transformations. Enterprise plans can be customized to your specific needs.

What languages are supported?

The Basic plan supports English only. Our Pro plan supports 10 major languages, including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, and Russian. Enterprise plans include all available languages (20+ and growing).

Do I own the content I create?

Yes, you retain full ownership of all content created using our platform. You're free to use, share, and distribute your avatar videos as you see fit, in accordance with our Terms of Service.
