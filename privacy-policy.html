Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 15th January 2025

Welcome to aiframe.ai (the "Service," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to safeguarding your personal information and your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and share information that we obtain from users of our website, application, and services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Registration Data

1.2 Video Content & Uploads

  • We collect and store videos that you upload or record within our platform for the purpose of providing AI-driven video optimizations. These videos may include audio and video content that can contain personal information if you include such information in your recordings.

1.3 Usage Data

  • We automatically collect certain usage-related data whenever you interact with our Services, such as log files, analytics data (e.g., page views, clicks, and feature usage), and device information (e.g., IP address, operating system, browser type).

1.4 Payment & Subscription Data

  • If you have a paid subscription, we may collect necessary billing information (handled by third-party payment processors) such as credit card details, transaction history, and subscription status.

1.5 Cookies & Similar Technologies

  • We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tools to remember user preferences, understand how users engage with our Services, and tailor advertisements or communications. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies; however, some features of our Services may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • We use your personal data (e.g., email, videos) to operate, maintain, and provide the features and functionality of our Services, including processing and optimizing videos.

2.2 Improvements & Analytics

  • We may use aggregated or anonymized data from your videos, usage patterns, or AI optimization results to enhance and develop our AI models and product features.

2.3 Communications

2.4 Billing & Account Management
